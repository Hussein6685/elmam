About Elmam

Definition of vision and goals

Vision

Elmam aspires to be the technical partner for establishments in the Kingdom by providing the best advice and guidance on government operations to establishments in a fully automated manner, which helps them make the best decisions and move towards success.

the goal

Elmam aims to help establishments and their employees in the field of government operations and human resources discover errors and observations in government operations, resolve them, and enable officials to make sound decisions to make the establishment.
